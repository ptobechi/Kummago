Who We Are

Chowbox is Kumago Food's first vertical product. It is a ready-to-eat meal service that caters to busy professionals in FCT Abuja. Our main target groups are Millennials and Gen Z's who are entrepreneurs, freelancers and 9-5ers, and as such, barely have to prepare their weekly or instant meals. We also provide meal deals that enable them to save more by buying more.
